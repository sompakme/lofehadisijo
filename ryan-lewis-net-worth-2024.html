Ryan Lewis’s Early Life

Ryan Lewis was born on March 25, 1988, in Spokane, Washington. He developed an interest in music at a young age and started playing guitar when he was just six years old. His passion for music led him to experiment with different genres and instruments, eventually leading him to produce his own music.

Beginning of His Career

Ryan Lewis’s professional career began in 2006 when he started working with various musicians and bands as a professional photographer and videographer. His breakthrough came in 2008 when he teamed up with Macklemore, and the duo released their first EP, “VS. EP,” in 2009.

Rise to Stardom

The duo’s debut studio album, “The Heist,” released in 2012, was a massive success. It reached the top of the US Billboard 200 and was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). The album’s hit single “Thrift Shop” topped the Billboard Hot 100 chart, making them the first duo in the chart’s history to achieve this feat with a debut single.

Awards and Achievements

Ryan Lewis and Macklemore’s success with “The Heist” earned them several awards, including four Grammy Awards in 2014. They won the awards for Best New Artist, Best Rap Album, Best Rap Song, and Best Rap Performance.

Other Ventures

Apart from music, Ryan Lewis has also ventured into other areas. He co-founded the 30/30 Project, an organization aimed at improving healthcare access in developing countries. He also launched his own record label, Bogart Records, in 2015.

Real Estate Investments
