The 25 Best Bracelets of 2024

September 26, 2024
blog

Maybe it's just me, but I feel like bracelets are a slept-on accessory. People tend to have their go-to necklaces and rings, but bracelets often go forgotten. Well, I'm here to draw your attention to the understated accessory. Bracelets can dramatically change your look for the better. You can throw on a simple tennis bracelet for subtle sparkle or go big and bold with a stack of extravagant bangles. Whatever your personal style, bracelets deserve a spot in your jewelry collection. 

But don't just take it from me—look towards the runways for proof that bracelets are a mainstay accessory. Bangles and cuffs emerged as one of this year's biggest jewelry trends from the Spring/Summer 2024 runaway, with Balmain, Schiaparelli, and Loewe all including chunky arm candy in their latest collections. Additionally, Gucci and Sacai proved that the big, bold chains from the fall 2023 shows hold strong through the new season.

In the list below, we've included the trendiest cuffs and bangles for you to try, but also expect to see more timeless styles like dainty links and charm bracelets. And if you lean towards more budget-friendly options, don't worry—we've included picks from our favorite affordable jewelry brands, too.  

Best Bangle Bracelets

Mazin Jewelry Lylah Bangle

This under-$100 pick is a minimalist's dream. It's a sleek and modern design, yet the added chain gives this bangle a touch of edginess so it's sure to stand out on your wrist. It's also adjustable so you won't have to worry about it being too big or too tight, and its 18k gold plating means it's sure to last you quite a while. 

Monica Vinader Engravable Signature Thin Bangle

An arm stack isn't complete without a simple bangle like this one. The cool geometric design of this bracelet gives a bit of edginess to an otherwise everyday piece. What makes this bangle extra special, though, is the option to have it engraved. We recommend pairing this one with all of your dainty silver bracelets, or you can mix metals for an even cooler vibe. 

Saint Moran Auguste Bangle

In addition to cuff bracelets, thick bangles like this one were all over the spring 2024 runways, so you know they are about to be everywhere. Even on its own, this Saint Moran bangle is the perfect statement-maker, but it would be even better stacked with other thick pieces. Bonus points if you style this over a slim-fitted sweater.  

Kate Spade New York Crystal Hinge Bangle

Some bracelets have the power to be the kind of wear-everyday piece and this is that bangle. In a similar style to the cult-favorite Cartier Love bracelet (albeit a much more affordable version), this pretty bangle can be added to your everyday wrist stack with ease. At just $58, this would also make for a great gift for any of your fashion-loving friends.

8 Other Reasons Wavy Bangle Bracelet

Chunky bangles are very much having a moment and I, for one, am here for it. This bangle may look simple, but it hits on the bracelet trend and is silver, which is particularly of the moment. For $59, why not grab two and really go for the cuffed-up look?

Best Cuff Bracelets

Karine Sultan Sculptural Cuff

Karine Sultan is a jewelry brand with a ton of options in the bracelets category and each one is unique and affordable. This cuff bracelet looks like a work of art that should be way more expensive. If you're wanting to dabble in the cuff bracelet trend, add this under-$100 piece to your cart. 

Mejuri Dôme Cuff Bracelet

For those who love timeless jewelry pieces but like to play with trends, I recommend this cuff bracelet from Mejuri. Made from a thick layer of 18k solid gold on a sterling silver base, this cuff bracelet is made to last. With its classic, simple design, this piece will fit in nicely with the rest of your gold jewelry collection. 

SOKO Bahari Band Cuff

This cuff may have a relatively simple design, but I promise this will amp up your outfit like no other accessory would. Its hammered texture gives a flashing eye-catching look, plus the two size options ensures you have a comfortable fit.

David Yurman Cable Classics Sterling Silver & 18K Yellow Gold Bracelet, 4mm

Some designer jewelry pieces are instantly recognizable and this cuff is one of them. David Yurman's signature rope-like textured pieces like this dainty cuff are sure to stand out among your wrist stack. Throw this bracelet on to instantly add some luxury to your look.

Missoma Molten Wave Cuff Bracelet

I can always count on Missoma to deliver cool pieces that still have a classic feel so you can wear them year after year. The best part is that they are high quality at a reasonable price point. This wavy bracelet, for example, looks on trend, but can easily be incorporated into any look.

Best Chain and Link Bracelets

PAVOI 14K Gold Plated Classic Tennis Bracelet Set

While this pick may not necessarily fit into the chain bracelet category, everyone needs a classic tennis bracelet in their collection. Real diamond tennis bracelets tend to be on the pricier side, but this Amazon bracelet set is a worthy substitute. I can personally attest to the quality of these bracelets—I've worn them each about 20 times and they're as sparkly and shiny as ever! You can't beat that $30 price tag, either. 

Justine Clenquet Paloma Chain-Link Bracelet (Was $80)

Get the look of two bracelets in one with this piece from Justine Clenquet. With this bracelet, you get the cool edginess from the mixed chain-link, plus a touch of glam with the added tennis-bracelet-esque strand. It's an effortless way to hit on two jewelry trends at once.

Jenny Bird Andi Slim Bracelet

If I had to name the one jewelry brand that's taken over the MC office, it would be Jenny Bird. The brand consistently launches trend-forward, statement-making pieces that are high-quality and so easy to wear. This link bracelet is the perfect example of the brand's classic designs with a refreshed twist.

Gorjana Brooklyn Bracelet

For an everyday piece you can turn to again and again, might we suggest Gorjana's Brooklyn bracelet? This timeless piece is inspired by the dainty watch bracelets made popular in the '80s and '90s. It's a retro take on a simple chain bracelet, and we couldn't be more obsessed. 

Luv AJ The Two-Toned Timepiece Bracelet

In case you haven't noticed, watches are fully back among the fashion crowd. If you're not quite ready to invest in a timepiece, get the next best thing: a watch-inspired bracelet. This bracelet has the same chain-link style as classic watches, plus the two-tone metal means it'll go with any other jewelry you have on.

Best Beaded Bracelets

BaubleBar Pisa Set of 3 Bracelets

Doubling (or in this case, tripling) up on basic pieces is never a bad idea when you're looking to build up your collection. This bracelet set may be simple, but don't underestimate their styling power. Mix, match, and stack these bracelets to your heart's content with the rest of your gold pieces to create your signature wrist stack. 

Harvest Moon Solid Gold Crystal Bracelet for Manifestation

This isn't just a pretty beaded bracelet. Made up of natural pink tourmaline, garnet, and red corundum gemstones, this bracelet can help you manifest more self love as you head into the new year. It doesn't hurt that it adds a nice pop of color, too.

Rellery Long Baroque Pearl Bracelet (Was $205)

This bracelet from Rellery takes a classic design and spins it on its head. Rather than your everyday pearl strand, this bracelet adds a dose of modernity with the statement-making baroque pearls. Each bracelet is one of a kind, too, thanks to the unique variations of the freshwater pearls. 

Dannijo Winny Mixed Bead Slider Bracelets in Gold Tone

What's better than one bracelet? Two bracelets! These beaded bracelets are filled with an assortment of multicolored gold-tone, resin, glass, and freshwater pearl beads that perfectly give off a boho vibe. Layer these with other beaded bracelets and you've got the ideal look for Coachella.

Eliou Elter Bracelet

Why wear a plain pearl or silver bracelet when you can wear them both? This handmade bracelet winds freshwater pearls around silver-plated beads to almost create an optical illusion. All in all, I'd say this bracelet is equal parts cool and classic.

Best Charm Bracelets

Pandora Barbed Wire Heart Bracelet Set

Think of this bracelet as the grown-up version of Pandora's signature clip and chain charm bracelets. This elevated version features a modern chain link design made of sterling silver, and comes with a cool barbed wire heart charm to kickstart your charm design.

AQUA Candy Multicolor Bracelet in 14K Gold Plated (Was $85)

How adorable is this charm bracelet? Candy-colored gems make up the tiny dangling charms so you'll get a glimpse of a sweet sparkling rainbow every time you swish your wrist. This dainty bracelet would be a great one to include in your stack for some added color and texture.

Oak and Luna Tyra Initial Medallion Bracelet

A piece of jewelry is always more special when it means something personal. With this bracelet, you can customize to your heart's content: add you and your besties initials, the initials of your kids, or your monogram. You can even include special symbols and make it truly special with an added diamond. In short, this is a one of a kind bracelet, guaranteed.

Elizabeth Cole Auryn Bracelet

As far as charm bracelets go, it doesn't get much better than this pick from Elizabeth Cole. The chunky curb chain features small heart charms with some rhinestones, so if you're looking to add some bling to your look (who isn't?), this is a subtle way to do so.

Missoma Jelly Heart Gemstone Charm Bracelet

If you haven't picked it up by now, heart charms are a major trend when it comes to bracelets. Each of the heart charms on this bracelet is a semi-precious gemstone so it's sure to be unique. Throw on this bracelet for an easy pop of color, and if you're more of a neutral fan, don't worry—there's a cool all-black option, too.
