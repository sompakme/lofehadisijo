Wizkid prostrates as he pays respect to Oba Elegushi (video)

August 06, 2024
blog

- A video of singer Wizkid at the palace of Oba Elegushi has surfaced online

- Wizkid was spotted prostrating as he paid his respects t the monarch

- Several internet users on Twitter commended the young superstar for his humility

Popular Nigerian singer Wizkid has warmed the hearts of members of the internet community, following a video of him that recently surfaced on social media.

It appeared the singer had paid a visit to the palace of popular monarch Oba Saheed Elegushi of Ikateland and he made sure to pay his due respect to the traditional ruler.

In the video shared by a Twitter user identified as @weedsom_ani, Wizkid was spotted waiting for the king to walk into the throne room.

The moment Oba Elegushi walked in, the music star immediately prostrated while the king acknowledged his presence.

Shortly after, he moved closer to the throne and received blessings from the king.
