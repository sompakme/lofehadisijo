In a recent interview with Shade 45, Wiz Khalifa explained the meaning of his upcoming album, O.N.I.F.C.

During the radio show he revealed that the acronym stands for "Only N*gga in First Class." His inspiration for this title came from Prodigy's debut album Head N*gga In Charge, "It’s inspired by Prodigy’s H.N.I.C.,” added Wiz. "When he dropped that, I felt like that sounded so strong."
