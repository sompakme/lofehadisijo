Skizzy Mars &amp; Prelow Link Up On New EP &quot;I Can't Take Me Nowhere&quot;

August 26, 2024
blog

Skizzy Mars has been consistently delivering dope music over the years, but this year, he's been going hard. The rapper released his latest project, Are You Okay? in April and has been pushing the project ever since. However, with a bit over a month left in 2018, the rapper is making sure he leaves the year on a good note. Today, he and Prelow team up for their new EP,  I Can't Take Me Nowhere.

Skizzy Mars teams up with Prelow for their joint effort, I Can't Take Me Nowhere. The new EP runs nine tracks deep with no credited features. The project includes the lead single, "R U Ok" which dropped earlier this month.

"If your ass isn't crying or horny after listening to our project then you might as well unfollow us now," reads a caption on Prelow's Instagram about their project.
