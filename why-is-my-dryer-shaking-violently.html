How does a faulty dryer motor sound?

A motor drives the blower and the drying drum in both gas and electric dryers. A rattling noise from a loose pulley, a high-pitched noise from a damaged bearing or bushing, or a humming noise from a poor winding or switch on the motor may all be caused by a malfunctioning motor.
