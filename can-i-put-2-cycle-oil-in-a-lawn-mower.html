How can you know if a lawn mower is two-stroke or four-stroke?

How to Spot a Small 2-Cycle Engine

A 2-cycle engine has a single fill port for both the engine oil and the engine gas.

You have a 4-cycle engine if the engine has two fill ports, one for gas and another for oil. In these engines, do not mix oil and gasoline.
