Katharine McPhee Lets Her Feelings Out About Being A Mom

August 19, 2024
blog

Katharine McPhee glowed in the sweet photo that she shared with her followers in early March 2021. According to the Daily Mail, the star used a blue baby carrier to hold her son close while they went for a walk. Fans caught a glimpse of the newborn's head as the new mom stood on the tree-lined sidewalk and smiled at the camera. "Just in case you were wondering..." McPhee wrote. "I love being a mommy!" She followed the sentiment with two crying emojis. 

While McPhee and husband David Foster have kept a low profile after the birth of their son, it appears as though the singer-actor has taken to motherhood while adjusting in the first few weeks post-partum. As a source divulged to E! around this time, "Katharine is having a wonderful time as a mother, and next up, she'll soon be promoting her new show Country Comfort on Netflix." For their part, McPhee and Foster are still keeping the baby's name quiet. But the Smash alum previously gushed to ET about expecting a boy, saying, "I always wanted a girl, that's always what I wanted — I always wanted both. Let's just be honest about that. But now that I'm having a boy, I'm like, 'Phew, wow, there's different things I need to worry about.' Versus the things I would be thinking about with a girl."

As for the infant himself, he has almost certainly struck the lullaby jackpot with his composer dad and songbird mom.
