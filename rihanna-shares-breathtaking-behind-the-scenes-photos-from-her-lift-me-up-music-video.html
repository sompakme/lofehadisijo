Rihanna Shares Breathtaking Behind-the-Scenes Photos from her Lift Me Up Music Video

July 24, 2024
blog

Rihanna is back to the music studio and back in the spotlight.

Yesterday, the singer and new mom shared dreamy behind-the-scenes photos on Instagram from her instantly viral "Lift Me Up" music video shoot. She looked stunning in an ivory gown with a corset bodice paired with an airy bomber jacket featuring a long train.

Rihanna was all glamour in the angelic look, accessorizing with silver and diamond rings, and a beaded necklace that fell down her bust. Her eye makeup matched the electric tone of her gown.

As she walked along the beach in the evening for the shoot, a lovely sunset above the water shone behind her.

She also shared behind-the-scenes clips of the making of the music video on YouTube. These included stunning shots of Rihanna dancing around a bonfire, letter her hair fly in the beach breeze, and singing toward the waters. It also included sweet shots of her talking and laughing with the production crew.

Rihanna released the official visual for her Wakanda Forever soundtrack single, "Lift Me Up," last Friday. The emotional three-and-a-half-minute video features scenes of her singing solo while strolling the beach, as well as scenes from the upcoming Black Panther sequel. The song is meant to be a tribute to late Black Panther actor Chadwick Boseman, who starred as T'Challa in the original blockbuster film and who sadly died in 2020 following a private battle with cancer.

"Lift Me Up" is Rihanna's first single in six years. She is set to make an even bigger music return early next year, when she headlines the Super Bowl 2023 Halftime Show.
