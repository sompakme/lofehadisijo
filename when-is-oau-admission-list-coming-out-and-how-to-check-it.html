When is OAU admission list coming out and how to check it?

August 12, 2024

Is Obafemi Awolowo University OAU admission list out? This article will provide you the information about how you can quickly check OAU admission list.

OAU admission 2018/2019

When is OAU admission list coming out? According to the latest information provided by the management of Obafemi Awolowo University, the list was out earlier this year. It only means that candidates can still check if their names are on the list of the admission. It’s quite simple, if you know how to do this!

How to check OAU admission?

If you need to check the admission, then you need to follow these steps to do that:

  • You will need to visit the official portal for the admission: eportal.oauife.edu.ng/admissions.php.
  • In the provided spaces, you will need to enter the year and JAMB UTME registration number.
  • If the data is entered correctly, then you just need to select “Submit”.
  • When you get OAU admission status, you will be required to visit JAMB CAPS. It’s needed to accept or reject the admission offer. Let’s take a look how you can do that!

How you can check AAUA admission

How to accept or reject JAMB result CAPS?

  • Select your mobile phone browser and open it.
  • Visit the official portal which is www.jamb.org.ng.
  • Select “E-Facility Account Login”.
  • You will need to enter your email and password to login.
  • From the menu list, you will need to check the Admission Status on CAPS. That’s how you will be redirected to the JAMB CAPS portal.
  • You will need to select 'Admission Status.'
  • Take a look at the admission status you are offered. If you see that you are on the list of OAU admission, then you choose “accept” if you want this admission or “reject” if you don’t.

That’s pretty all that you can do with OAU admission. If you don’t have the admission proposal on the website or in your JAMB CAPS, then your last option would be contacting OAU and ask directly if there is no mistake in the admission list.
