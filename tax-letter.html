Tax Letter

September 06, 2024
blog

Following up his remix to “Don't Run” with Young MA, Fabolous, Don Q, & Dave East, fast-rising Brooklyn rapper Casanova decides to return to the scene this week & share a new single & accompanying video called “Tax Letter,” which serves as tribute to his good friend & fellow podcaster Taxstone, who was arrested last month for allegedly being the culprit behind Troy Ave’s shooting last May.

Produced by GQ Beats, look for the track to see life on Casanova’s upcoming debut mixtape Be Safe Tho, due out this Friday March 17th. Take a listen and let us know what you think.
