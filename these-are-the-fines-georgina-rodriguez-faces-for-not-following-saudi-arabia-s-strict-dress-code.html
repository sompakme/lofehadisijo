These are the fines Georgina Rodriguez faces for not following Saudi Arabia's strict dress code

July 10, 2024
blog

The fact that Cristiano Ronaldo signed for Al Nassr has meant that his whole family have had to move to Saudi Arabia and get used to a very different culture.

This might be more difficult for Georgina Rodriguez, Cristiano Ronaldo's partner, because of her fashion style and the way she often shows off her looks, in public and for social media.

But, the model and influencer needs to adapt to the strict customs of Saudi Arabia, a Muslim country with very clear rules, especially for women, on how to dress.

Georgina Rodriguez wears an abaya, as well as inappropriate dresses

Georgina Rodriguez has been seen in public wearing the abaya, a simple and loose typical Saudi dress, one associated with women in the Muslim religion.

However, Georgina is Georgina and, therefore, she has wanted to keep up her personal style too, using low-cut, short garments or ones with slits in the legs.

That might go against the public decency law that exists in the nation.

"It is recommended that, in public, you wear loose-fitting clothes that cover up to the elbow and below the ankle," the Visit Saudi tourism website explains.

Although there is no complete list of prohibited garments, it is understood that women can't wear miniskirts, shorts, low-cut tops, halter tops, straps or ripped trousers.

Possible fine for Georgina Rodriguez

With Georgina Rodriguez at risk of breaking this rule, this could bring her a financial penalty.

The first time a visitor commits such an infraction, he or she must pay a fine of 100 riyals, which is equivalent to about 25 euros.

On the second occasion the fine is 200 riyals, just under 50 euros.

That might just be a drop in the ocean for Cristiano Ronaldo, considering how much he is being paid by Al Nassr, but he and Georgina Rodriguez will certainly want to keep out of trouble.
