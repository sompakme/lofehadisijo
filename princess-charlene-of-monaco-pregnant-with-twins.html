Princess Charlene of Monaco Pregnant With Twins

September 10, 2024
blog

Media Platforms Design Team

Not one but two royal babies are on the way for Prince Albert II and Princess Charlene of Monaco. After announcing Charlene's pregnancy in May, the royal couple confirmed the news of twins in an interview with Hello! Magazine this morning while in New York.

While Prince Albert already has two children from previous relationships, this will mark the couple's first children together and the first pregnancy for 36-year-old Charlene. Both Prince Albert and Princess Charlene have expressed carrying on the late Princess Grace Kelly's hands-on maternal style and overall legacy, in an interview with People.

The newest Monaco royal babies are expected to arrive this December.
