Much is known about former NFL wide receiver Antonio Brown. Once among the best receivers in football during his prime, the 34-year-old is now out of the football bubble and is living the high life.

Well, sort of. His ex-fiance Chelsie Kyriss, along with Brown himself, has made headlines for the wrong reasons recently. The former Tampa Bay Buccaneers receiver posted an explicit picture of himself and Kyriss on his Snapchat, which caused his account to be suspended.

Many people have been wondering exactly who Kyriss is. Well, luckily for you, we have it covered.

Born in Ohio back in 1989, Chelsie Kyriss is the mother to three of Antonio Brown's children. She also has another two from a previous relationship. Talk about a big household! She is also a former customer service rep and a former teacher, according to The Sun.

Kyriss and Brown were engaged back in 2020, but at the time of writing, it appears that the pair have gone their separate ways.

Every relationship has its ups and downs, and Kyriss and Brown's is no different. Both have accused each other of being unfit parents in what has become a rather hostile relationship, and the latest incident with Brown's Snapchat appears to be the final straw.

Given what has recently taken place between the pair, it is not known if they are still together, although an educated guess would say they aren't.

Antonio Brown's career ended in a sour manner

Incidents for the former NFL receiver have come thick and fast. His run-in with the Pittsburgh Steelers, when he live-streamed Mike Tomlin giving a post-game speech, and then his helmet fiasco with the Las Vegas Raiders are some of the examples.

Then, when in Tampa Bay, he was a model citizen for a year before he walked out on the Buccaneers during the third quarter of a game against the New York Jets.

We haven't seen him on an NFL field since.

With all the incidents that seem to follow the 34-year-old, plus the events with his ex-fiance Kyriss, who publicly said he needs help, Brown is never far away from the headlines.
