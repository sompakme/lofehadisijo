All Altar of Lilith maps and locations in Diablo 4

September 26, 2024
blog

Diablo 4’s five unique regions offer a total of 160 collectibles for players to find: the Altars of Lilith. Each one of these Altars is crucial for serious players to track down, as they offer a wide variety of bonuses, including permanent stat boosts and bonus Paragon Points. But all of them are hidden in clever places, making tracking them down a bit difficult — that’s where we come in.

In this Diablo 4 guide, we’ll tell you everything you need to know about the Altars of Lilith in the Fractured Peaks, Scosglen, the Dry Steppes, Kehjistan, and Hawezar.

Our interactive map can give you a bird’s-eye view of the locations for all 160 Altars of Lilith in Sanctuary, allowing you to quickly scan the continent and find where you need to go.

Below, in our detailed walkthrough, you’ll find our numbered maps showing the locations of the Altars of Lilith in the Fractured Peaks, Scosglen, the Dry Steppes, Kehjistan, and Hawezar. You’ll also find links out to our full walkthroughs for each in-game region, which will show you the exact location for each Altar of Lilith you’re looking for.

The Fractured Peaks Altars of Lilith locations map

There are 28 Altars of Lilith scattered throughout the Fractured Peaks — where you’ll play through Act 1 of Diablo 4’s campaign. 24 of the Altars in this region will give all characters on your account +2 to one of the four core stats (Dexterity, Intelligence, Strength, and Willpower). The other four will increase the maximum number of Murmuring Obols you can carry at once by five (a total of +20).

If you need help finding all the individual Altars of Lilith in the Fractured Peaks, read our walkthrough guide specific to the region.
