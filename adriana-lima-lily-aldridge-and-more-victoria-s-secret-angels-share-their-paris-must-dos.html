Adriana Lima, Lily Aldridge and more Victoria's Secret Angels share their Paris must-dos

July 31, 2024
blog
    Sights, (delicious) delights and lights! The Victoria's Secret Angels are taking flight to Paris for the brand's annual fashion show, which airs December 5 at 10 p.m. EST on CBS. HELLO! US chatted with some of the supermodels ahead of the highly-anticipated show and gathered their must-dos for the City of Light.Photo: Courtesy of Victoria's Secret Adriana Lima"Start your day having breakfast at Angelina (hot chocolate is a must). Then visit the Louvre Museum (make sure you see Mona Lisa). Lunch time at Ladurée ( they do have great salads). The afternoon at the Picasso museum and finally dinner at the traditional Chez l'Ami Louis." Photo: Getty Images/Instagram Lily Aldridge"Going to Un Dimanche à Paris and getting a cup of my favorite hot chocolate. It is out of this world!" Photo: Getty Images/Instagram Jasmine Tookes"One of the things I love to do is go to the little beauty stores, like the pharmacies. They have the best beauty products and moisturizers." Photo: Instagram/@jasminetookes Alessandra Ambrosio“There [are] so many fun things to do in Paris. From visiting museums, shopping and just taking a leisurely stroll by Seine or Tuileries Garden, but probably my favorite is French food. A restaurant that is a must is Caviar Kaspia. Potato with caviar is to die for.”Photo: Getty Images/Instagram Martha Hunt“Paris must-dos are walking around the Tuileries. Also going to Musée d'Orsay. That’s my favorite museum there. I can never say it right, but it’s my favorite," she laughed. "Walking around the Marais and St. Germaine. They’re both beautiful neighborhoods.”Following the show, Martha added, “Traditionally we have a pizza party, so I’m hoping somebody is already on that figuring out whose going to find pizza in Paris for us.”Photo: Getty Images/Instagram Elsa Hosk"Paris must-dos is eat lots of croissants. Walk around the streets. I like to look at the architecture and just enjoy Paris." Photo: Getty Images/Instagram
