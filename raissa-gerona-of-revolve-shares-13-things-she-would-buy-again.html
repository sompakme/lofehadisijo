Raissa Gerona of Revolve Shares 13 Things She Would Buy Again

July 06, 2024
blog

1

LPA Acosta Dress

Credit: Revolve

"Pia makes the best vacation-wear, hands-down, and her dresses especially are a go-to for me. She always has an interesting color or cut that makes her looks stand out, so whenever I travel, I make sure I have a few of her pieces."

2

Dior Small Book Tote

Now 11% Off

Credit: Dior

"I have a big 'Raissa' Dior tote bag I use for all things work. It’s been my go-to for a while. Bags for me have always been pretty special. As I built my career, I began collecting, and now to commemorate certain moments, such as a special trip, or career milestone—when we went public a couple of years ago, for example—I will invest in a new bag that becomes a keepsake, and something I will eventually share with my daughter."

3

Frank Gehry Desk and Chair

Credit: 1st Dibs

"We are starting to go back to the office, but I think working from home will always have a place going forward—and it’s so important to have a great setup you’re excited to 'go to' every day!"

4

The Row Lazco Pant

Now 21% Off

Credit: FWRD

"We just launched The Row RTW, and I am obsessed with it all—it’s been a long-time coming for FWRD, and we are so excited to finally have it in our assortment. I plan to wear these Lazco pants with a bandeau top and the brand’s 'bare' sandals all summer."

5

Raquel Vidal and Pedro Paz Lebes Hueso Stoneware Vase

Credit: Claude Home

"I am renovating a new home, and obsessed with everything home—Claude Home’s founder, Maggie [Holladay], brings such a clear point of view and relatable fashion perspective to her curation. I am always inspired by what she’s showcasing."

6

Sami Miro Vintage Asymmetrical Long Sleeve Mini Dress

Credit: Revolve

"I love how Sami combines sustainability and fashion together to make the coolest womenswear pieces. The asymmetrical dress is perfect for a night out and you can’t go wrong with the matching sweatsuit as an everyday essential."

7

818 Tequila Blanco 750ml Bottle

Credit: Total Wine

"This is my go-to for serving drinks after work on summer Fridays!"

8

Celine Technical Jersey Bra Top

Credit: Celine

"I love to have a dressed down vibe, while still feeling feminine during the daytime. This bra top has become an easy way for me to feel dressed up, since it's camp high fashion, but in an athleisure way that doesn’t make me feel fussy."

9

Celine Cropped Shirt

Credit: Celine

"[I layer the bra top with] the menswear cropped button-down. It gives a nod to business."

10

Summer Fridays Jet Lag Mask

Credit: Sephora

"[It's] so amazing to see my friends reach their four year anniversary…the products are such thoughtful additions to the skincare universe. The Jet Lag Mask is a favorite because hydration is key for when I’m using beauty products."

11

Ch4rm Pink Petals Top

Credit: Ch4rm

"I am really loving a new designer I discovered via Up Next Designer, Ch4rm, and plan to wear one of the label’s incredibly artistic tops at Revolve Festival this spring!"

12

Mad Et Len Bloc Potpourri

Credit: Garde Shop

"An incredibly chic housewarming gift. Filled with either amber stones or lava rocks, these are infused with scents from the South of France, and to amp up the fragrance potency you add a few drops at a time from a little bottle of perfumed oil. Instead of self-care, it’s home-care."

Versace S/S 2005 Plunging Wrap Crop Top

Credit: 1st Dibs

"Also for Festival, I love the idea of playing with vintage when I’m not wearing Revolve or FWRD—which is most of the time! This Versace top is one I have had my eyes on at 1stDibs and it pairs well with Levi’s—the Festival staple!"

Halie LeSavage is the fashion commerce editor at Harper's BAZAAR. Her style reporting covers everything from reviewing the best designer products to profiling emerging brands and designers. Previously, she was the founding retail writer at Morning Brew and a fashion associate at Glamour.
