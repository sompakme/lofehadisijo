WWE RAW November 27th, 2023 Preview: Confirmed Matches, Start Time and How To Watch

July 09, 2024
blog

WWE RAW might see a huge viewership boost for their November 27th episode. Anything can happen in WWE, and they proved that in the Allstate Arena for Survivor Series after CM Punk made his return to the company.

Tonight’s WWE RAW, broadcasting live from Nashville, TN at the Bridgestone Arena, promises to be an eventful one in the aftermath of Saturday’s Survivor Series. Ringside News will be here with all the updates before the show, and comprehensive coverage once everything kicks off.

The longest running weekly episodic television show in history will air at 8:00 PM EST on the USA Network. They also have a few things on deck already for their Survivor Series fallout.

One of the most anticipated highlights of the evening is the Raw return of CM Punk, who hasn’t graced the flagship show since 2014. We will have to see what the Second City Savior does as he re-inserts himself into WWE’s creative puzzle.

The event’s card includes notable matches such as a Tag Team Turmoil number one contenders match featuring The New Day, Indus Sher, Alpha Academy, The Creed Brothers, Imperium, and DIY. The winners of that match will get a shot at Judgment Day’s Undisputed Tag Team Titles.

Additionally, Bronson Reed will face off against Ivar. Natalya and Tegan Nox are also set to challenge Piper Niven and Chelsea Green for the WWE Women’s Tag Team Championship.

Advertised stars for the night include WWE World Heavyweight Champion Seth Rollins, Becky Lynch, WWE Women’s World Champion Rhea Ripley, Sami Zayn, and WWE Intercontinental Champion GUNTHER.

Only time will tell what WWE fans are talking about on Tuesday morning. This could be a big time for WWE, as they have an entire month to build stories toward the Royal Rumble in Tropicana Field on January 27th, 2024.

WWE RAW November 27th, 2023 Card:

CM Punk returns to WWE RAW

Randy Orton to appear

The New Day vs. Indus Sher vs. Alpha Academy vs. The Creed Brothers vs. Imperium vs. DIY – Tag Team Turmoil number one contenders match

Bronson Reed vs. Ivar

Natalya and Tegan Nox vs Piper Niven and Chelsea Green – WWE Women’s Tag Team Championship Match
