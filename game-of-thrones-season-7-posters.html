    In addition to debuting a second official trailer for Game of Thrones Season 7, HBO also unveiled a new set of character posters. In case you haven't noticed yet, Winter is here. The new images feature 12 familiar faces from the GoT universe like you've never seen them before: tinted pale blue with icy blue eyes—just like a White Walker. (Notice the snow, too.) The edits might just be an aesthetic choice, but they're also a reminder than anyone is at the hands of the Winter next season.
