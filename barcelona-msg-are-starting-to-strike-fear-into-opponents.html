Barcelona: MSG are starting to strike fear into opponents

September 17, 2024
blog

Barcelona All three scored against Mallorca

Lionel Messi, Luis Suarez and Antoine Griezmann keep leaving their mark on opposition teams.

All three points of the Barcelona trident scored against Real Mallorca on Saturday night.

First it was Griezmann, who ended his LaLiga Santander scoring drought going back to October.

Then it was the newly crowned Ballon d'Or winner Messi, who ended up with a hat-trick.

Finally, it was the Uruguayan, who pulled off a stunning backheel finish to beat Manolo Reina.

This was the third time each member of the MSG attack had scored in one game, with their previous two victims being Eibar and Borussia Dortmund.

MSG have scored 25 of Barcelona's 41 goals in LaLiga Santander this season.

We already knew how well Messi and Suarez combined on the pitch, but now Griezmann is beginning to look like the final piece to the puzzle.

The Frenchman is improving his relationship with both Messi and Suarez and is starting to contribute frequently, whether scoring or assisting.

The three forwards are also getting closer away from football and they often go out for dinner together.

Griezmann's recent form has silenced the calls for Neymar to return to the Camp Nou - for now.

"We're here to understand each other and it's getting better each match," Griezmann said.

"Furthermore, it was a special night for Messi [to celebrate] for sixth Ballon d'Or, something incredible, and we wanted to make it amazing for him."

Barcelona's attack is becoming a nightmare for opposition defences to face and they're striking fear into the rest of LaLiga Santander.
