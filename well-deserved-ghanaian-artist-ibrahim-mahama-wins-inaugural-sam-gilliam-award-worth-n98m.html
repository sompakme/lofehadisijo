Well Deserved: Ghanaian Artist Ibrahim Mahama Wins Inaugural Sam Gilliam Award worth N98m

August 03, 2024
blog
  • Revered installation artist Ibrahim Mama has been awarded the prestigious Sam Gilliam Award
  • The honour comes with a substantial N98m prize and an opportunity to be featured in a public programme at the Dia Art Foundation
  • Mahama highlighted his achievement on his social media accounts and ignited excitement among his supporters and admirers

Ghanaian artist Ibrahim Mahama has won the inaugural Sam Gilliam Award, accompanied by a $75,000 prize and a feature in a public programme at Dia this year.

Established in 202 year by the Dia Art Foundation and the Sam Gilliam Foundation, the award seeks to preserve and honour the legacy of the late Sam Gilliam by giving out prizes annually through 2033.

Ibrahim Mahama was first introduced to Gilliam’s important work as a student by his mentor, Kąrî'kạchä Seid’ou.
