Kelsey Hollis Biography & Early Life

Kelsey Hollis is a native of Warner Robins, Georgia. She was born into a family of Afro-Americans with a dream to become a famous personality in the world. When she was in Houston County High School she became active in doing work related to modeling. When she reached 14 years of age this amazing girl became a founder of the non-profit organization “TAABS: Teaching Autism Awareness by Siblings.” She founded it because her brother Jeremy Hollis is suffering from Autism.

At the age of 15, she got the opportunity to pass bill SB118. This bill was made to provide $35,000 in insurance coverage per year to people suffering from Autism. Kelsey met Georgia legislators for this bill. Later on March 31, 2015, this bill was passed. You may be amazed to know that when Kelsey was 16 she launched her own beauty pageant titled “Miss Queen of Summer”. She is now the founder and director of this pageant.

She Studies at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville where she has been doing her internship interned for VFL Films and the SEC Network and one day plans a career in sports psychology. Her other dream is to open her own company where she can be a traveling sports psychologist.

Next in 2021 Kelsey who was already getting fame for his different participation competed in Miss Tennessee Competition. At the Miss Tennessee beauty pageant, she was named the third runner-up along with winning the preliminary talent award, and an overall talent award.

Then in the same year, that is August of 2021 Kelsey became the Miss Capital City Beauty pageant winner and got the key to open the doors to competing at the Miss Georgia beauty pageant once again.
