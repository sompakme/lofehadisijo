FCT, Abuja - The minister of Aviation and Aerospace Development of Nigeria, Festus Keyamo, said Emirates Airline would resume flight operations to Nigeria on October 1, 2024.

Keyamo said Emirates Airline officially made the announcement on Thursday, May 16.

Emirates to resume flight operations in Nigeria

The minister disclosed this via his X handle (formerly known as Twitter) @fkeyamo on Thursday, May 16.
