Abby Rao: Who is the TikTok star with whom Dillon Danis is sparking relationship rumors?

August 14, 2024
blog

Dillon Danis possibly has a new girlfriend following his defeat to Logan Paul on the undercard of Tommy Fury vs KSI, during which his trash-talk approach was to go after Paul's fiancee online.

He's supposedly dating Abby Rao, who is an Instagram model with 2.3 million followers. The blonde bombshell is aged 26, making her four years younger than Danis.

She briefly dated RiceGum, a YouTuber, between 2018 and 2019 before breaking up after a year.

She's now in the news after snuggling up to Danis in a now-deleted post on X, formerly Twitter with Conor McGregor's training partner calling her 'sweetest girl'.

Danis did, however, clarify that the pair are currently friends via his Instagram Stories by writing, 'One of my good friends! Good girl! @abbyrao' accompanied by a photo of them at a restaurant.

Danis gets his own medicine

Prior to his disqualification against Logan Paul for which he tried to attack the personality between rounds as he was outclassed, his build-up had largely surrounding going after Paul's fiancee, Nina Agdal, on X.com.

As a result, the public wasted no time giving him his own style of banter back by posting photos of Rao with previous partners in his comment section.

That could have contributed to Danis deleting the post of them, which would be a hilarious sense of irony that he couldn't take what he was keen to dish out.

Danis cut from Bellator

Following the loss to Paul, in which he looked like a complete amateur despite being the one with the reputation as an actual fighter, Danis lost his contract with Bellator an MMA competition.

He had already had to pay a $100,000 penalty to the group for going to fight Paul in the contest which was streamed by Bellator's sponsors' rivals, DAZN and Misfits Boxing. They compete against Showtime.

"Dillon Danis is a free agent," Ariel Helwani wrote on X. "Bellator officially released Danis this weekend, sources say."
