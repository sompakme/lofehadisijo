Match Stopped Early Due To Apparent Injury During Day 1 RAW

August 19, 2024
blog

WWE has big show planned for their January 1st episode, as they kick things into high gear. This will include a night of unexpected events at the Day 1 RAW, but now we know one thing to expect for next week.

Ringside News provided comprehensive coverage of WWE RAW’s January 1st’s Day One episode. You never know what can happen, and this includes injuries when you least expect it.

Kofi Kingston and Jey Uso took on Giovanni Vinci and Ludwig Kaiser. The match was going well, but then everything was stopped.

Giovanni Vinci went down on the apron, and the match was stopped suddenly. Ringside doctors flooded Vinci, and then the referee called for the match to be stopped. This was a sudden way to finish the match, as Kofi Kingston and Jey Uso were handed the win.

During the commentary, Michael Cole emphasized the importance of prioritizing the safety of their talent. As of now, the details regarding what occurred with Giovanni Vinci remain unclear, but the situation was significant enough to lead to the cancellation of the match.

After the match, Giovanni Vinci was seen walking out on his own. This was certainly a shocking way for this match to go down. We will keep an eye out for more updates.
