FAQs

Does FitFlop have free shipping?

FitFlop does have free shipping for orders over $129. If your order is less than this, standard shipping costs $8.95. You can also check here at Marie Claire for any free shipping codes.  

Can I get a newsletter discount from FitFlop online?

Yes, FitFlop gives you 15% off your first full priced order when you sign up for the FitFlop newsletter. You can sign up online with your email address—it couldn’t be easier.  

Can I get a student discount code at FitFlop?

Yes, students can get 15% off at FitFlop with a valid student discount code. You can access yours by navigating to the ‘Student Discount’ section on FitFlop’s website and confirming your student status via GoCertify. 

Does FitFlop have sales?

Like most retailers, FitFlop does have a dedicated ‘Sale’ section on its website. Our savings experts have previously noted up to 40% off with the sale. You can also shop key dates such as Black Friday and Boxing Day for strong discounts.  

Is there a FitFlop store near me?

If you’d like to visit in-store, FitFlop has a helpful ‘Find a Retailer’ list on its website. You can usually find the brand at popular department stores such as Bloomingdale’s and Macy’s.  

What is the returns policy at FitFlop?

FitFlop has free returns within 28 days of delivery, if you are not 100% happy. The brand simply asks that you try the shoes on indoors first. Shoes will only be accepted for returns in their original condition and tags, without being worn outdoors. As FitFlop have gone paperless, you will need to download your returns label from their website online.  

Hints and Tips

Newsletter discounts: Sign up for the FitFlop email newsletter to get 15% off your first order. Your order will need to contain full-priced items to qualify. You’ll also be sent new product updates, brand news, and sales alerts. You can also tailor your marketing preferences by selecting ‘women’ or ‘men’. 

Holiday sales: Save on stylish yet comfortable footwear by shopping the FitFlop holiday sales. Our savings experts have previously noted up to 40-50% off during last year’s Black Friday sales. You can also shop key holidays such as Christmas and Summer for more savings.

You can access your discount through the ‘Keyworker Discount’ section, then simply log into GoCertify. Other keyworkers, seniors, and students can also get 15% off full priced orders.
