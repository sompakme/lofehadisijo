Young Thug Swoons Over NBA YoungBoy Photo

September 24, 2024
blog

This year has marked the arrival of YoungBoy Never Broke Again. The young star has been on the rise in the music world, consistently dropping off music since the beginning of the year. Until Death Call My Name and Master The Day Of Judgment both have great replay value as neither of the projects have fallen out of rotation. He's already got a large list of famous fans too as he's collaborated heavily with Kevin Gates and Young Thug in the past. Thugger has definitely taken a liking to the Baton Rouge up-and-comer as he's already crowning him as one of the GOATs (which is definitely a little premature). 

Thug posted a photo of YoungBoy to his Instagram account as he showed off his chain for the camera. The On The Rvn artist has always been pretty sexually ambiguous in public as many have questioned him over his orientation in the past. His on-again-off-again girlfriend Karlae likely has some insight on that but Jeffery will continue to keep people guessing with his most recent post. Adding a bunch of hearts and goat emojis, Thug takes his friendship with the 19-year-old to the next level.

Young Thug was a guest on YoungBoy's "Permanent Scar" from 4Respect 4Freedom 4Loyalty 4WhatImportant and we're bound to hear more work from the duo in the future.
