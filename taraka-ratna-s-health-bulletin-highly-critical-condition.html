Taraka Ratnas Health Bulletin : Highly Critical Condition

August 25, 2024
blog

Here is the health bulletin of actor Nandamuri Taraka Ratna who suffered massive cardiac arrest on 27th January. The health bulletin was released by a team of specialists at the Narayana Hrudayalaya hospital who are treating the actor.

As per the health bulletin, Taraka Ratna’s health is very critical and he is on a maximal support. NH team of doctors who visited Kuppam earlier on 27th Jan to evaluate Taraka Ratna’s condition found he have “Anterior Wall Myocardial Infraction with Balloon Angioplasty, on Intra-Aortic Ballon Pump (IABP) and Vasoactive support. He was shifted to Bangalore hospital at around 1 am on 28th January via road.

Taraka Ratna was further evaluated by higher level diagnostics which showed his condition was very critical due to cardiogenic shock after Myocardial Infraction.

Currently, Taraka Ratna is under the care of multi-disciplinary clinical team involving cardiologists, intensifiers and other specialists. The hospital appealed the visitors not to turn up in order to provide privacy and uninterrupted treatment to Taraka Ratna.
