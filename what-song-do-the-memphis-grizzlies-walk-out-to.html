The Memphis Grizzlies Walkout Song: A Never-Ending Mystery (But Mostly Hype)

Ah, the Memphis Grizzlies walkout song. A question that has perplexed fans, confused opponents, and probably kept a few DJs up at night scrambling for the right banger. Unlike some teams with their tried-and-true anthems, the Grizzlies keep things fresh, funky, and occasionally downright puzzling.

Here Today, Gone Tomorrow (But Maybe Back Next Week)

One minute you might be treated to a Memphis legend like Project Pat or Three 6 Mafia getting the crowd crunk (it's Memphis, what do you expect?), the next you're head-bobbing to a national chart-topper from Moneybagg Yo or Future. The Grizzlies are the chameleons of the walkout world, adapting their soundtrack to fit the vibe.

There have even been rumors (whispers, really) of surprise guest appearances! Rapper NLE Choppa reportedly walked out the team with a live performance in 2023, smoke machines and all. Now that's an entrance!

The Case for "The Law of Recognition"

Hold up, hold up. Don't think this is just musical mayhem. There has been a recent contender stealing the show: "The Law of Recognition" by Memphis-made artist Kyslingo. This infectious hip-hop track with a motivational message has become a fan favorite. It's got that Memphis grit mixed with a winning attitude, making it a perfect fit for the Grizzlies' swagger. Will it become the official anthem? Only time (and the Grizzlies' ever-evolving musical tastes) will tell.

But hey, that's the beauty of it, right? The Grizzlies keep us guessing, the energy stays high, and the playlists are always on point. It's a win-win for everyone (except maybe opposing teams who can't predict what kind of sonic assault they're about to face).

How to Get Pumped Up Like a Grizzly (Even Without Knowing the Walkout Song)

Here's the real takeaway: You don't need to know the exact song to get hyped for a Grizzlies game. Just channel your inner Memphis and:

  • Blast some Bluff City classics: Throw on some Project Pat, Yo Gotti, or even some Al Green for some soulful inspiration.
  • Practice your roar: The Grizzlies' crowd is legendary. Get those vocal cords warmed up for some serious cheering.
  • Wear your teal with pride: Show your team spirit and represent the Grind City.

Grizzlies Walkout Song FAQs:

How do I find out what the walkout song will be for the next game?

Unfortunately, there's no guaranteed way. The Grizzlies like to keep things under wraps. But you can always check the team's social media or tune into the pre-game show for hints.

Can I request a song for the walkout?

Probably not, but hey, it never hurts to try! Tweet at the Grizzlies or show up with a giant sign expressing your musical desires. (Just don't be offended if they don't take you up on your Barry Manilow suggestion.)

What if I hate the walkout song?

That's okay! The beauty of basketball (and music) is that there's something for everyone. Just focus on the amazing plays and Ja Morant's gravity-defying dunks.

Is there a walkout song for away games?

Nope! The Grizzlies travel light (musically speaking) for away games. But hey, that just means you get to experience the unique pre-game hype of the opposing team's arena.

Will the Grizzlies ever have a permanent walkout song?

Only the basketball gods (and the Grizzlies' management) know. But for now, the mystery and ever-changing soundtrack are all part of the fun.
