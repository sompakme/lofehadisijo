Gospel Singer Israel Houghton, Wife Adrienne Baillon Welcome First Child Together via Surrogacy

September 21, 2024
blog
  • TV host Adrienne Baillon and her husband Israel Houghton had been trying to have a baby for the past five years, and finally, their prayers were answered
  • The actress shared a long post accompanied by a photo of herself and her hubby holding a newborn baby and announced they have been blessed with a son, albeit through surrogacy
  • Their followers congratulated them for welcoming a newborn and wished them well as they embark on parenting

US gospel singer Israel Houghton and his TV host wife Adrienne Baillon have joyfully welcomed their first child together via a surrogate mum.

Adrienne Baillon: We prayed for this child

Baillon wrote a long post on her Instagram page narrating how the journey to have their own baby was filled with ups and downs while thanking God for blessing them with a child.
