NFL star Travis Kelce and pop sensation Taylor Swift, who have been dating for the past few weeks, are facing the challenge of maintaining their relationship as Swift embarks on her international leg of The Eras Tour in Argentina.

During a recent dinner, Swift mentioned she won't be attending many of her boyfriend's games as she is in "work mode" preparing for her tour, but Kelce plans to attend some part of it.

Celebrity psychic Inbaal Honigman shared insights with The Mirror, suggesting that Kelce "can't bear" to be apart from Swift as she gets into "work mode."

Tarot card revelations

Honigman conducted a Tarot card reading and revealed that their relationship's longevity depends on finding each other interesting.

She emphasized that as long as they find each other creative, romantic, and stimulating, the distance, wealth, and location won't matter in their relationship.

"The Prince of Cups Tarot card tells us that Travis will cope with Taylor's international leg of the Eras Tour the best way he knows how - by making sure his passport is valid and flying out to meet his superstar girlfriend whenever he can," Honigman noted.

"This card is romantic and passionate, and at this stage of their relationship, Travis just can't physically bear to be parted from his Mrs for more than a few days at a time. Whatever it costs, he'll fly out to see her.

"Whether their relationship would stand the test of time, is totally unrelated to their relationship being long-distance at times.

"The Moon Tarot card, which represents travel and progress as well as illusion and delusion, says that they'll both travel to be with each other as much as they can, but when they can't, they just can't and they overcome it. No pressure."
