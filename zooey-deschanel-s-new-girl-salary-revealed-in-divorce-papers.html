Zooey Deschanels New Girl Salary Revealed in Divorce Papers

August 13, 2024
blog

Move over Suze Orman. Hollywood has an new budget balancer, and it’s … Zooey Deschanel?

The New Girl actress and singer filed to divorce rocker-husband Ben Gibbard on Dec. 27 after more than two years of marriage, citing irreconciliable differences. Court documents, via TMZ, reveal the 31-year-old’s net worth and arguably thrifty celebrity approach to finances.

VIDEO: Zooey Deschanel And Joseph Gordon-Levitt Sing “What Are You Doing New Year’s Eve?”

According to the papers, Deschanel earns a monthly paycheck of $95,000 for starring in the freshman Fox sitcom. She’s listed $1,578,000 in the bank, $1,645,000 in stocks and $693,300 in property, bringing her total net worth to $3,916,300.

Here is a partial list of how she spends it, per Deschanel’s breakdown of average monthly expenses totaling $22,550: telephone, cell phone and email ($300); dining out ($500); laundry ($600); utilities ($800); groceries and household supplies ($1,000); clothes ($2000). And Deschanel gives almost as much to charity each month as she spends on clothes — a generous $1,500 per month.

But here’s the kicker: Deschanel has three credit cards — American Express, Visa and MasterCard — on which she lists an impressive balance of zero.
