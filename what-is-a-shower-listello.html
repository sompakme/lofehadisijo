As a result, what exactly does Listello mean?

Listello is an Italian word that literally translates as lath. When it comes to construction, a lath is a thin strip of wood or metal that runs horizontally and is used to support another building material, most often plaster. Most significantly, a listello creates a visual break in a big stretch of field tile by separating it into smaller sections.
